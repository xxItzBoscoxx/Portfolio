Rhiannon Super is a user experience designer, storyteller, and game developer based in Long Island, New York.


She makes it her mission to go on adventures each day - whether those be in the real world, a video game, movie, book, or in her own imagination.


Her passion is creating interactive stories for others to enjoy. She wants to captivate people with her narratives and connect with them on an emotional level. She has been successful in doing so through various projects such as Riskmance and Witch’s Aventure, where she contributed to the games’ worldbuilding through ideation and programming using software like GameMaker Studio 2 and Unity. Rhiannon is excited to begin working with collaborative teams to learn new things, experience different views, and to make her visions come to life.
